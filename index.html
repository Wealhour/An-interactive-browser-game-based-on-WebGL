Cube Man Adventure

After selecting the difficulty, click play ↓↓↓



The cube man entered the multiverse accidentally, he needs to find exit as soon as he can.

Arrows or WASD of keyboard can control cube man movement.

There are many interesting things and beautiful scenery in this world.

Please enjoy your adventure

Hint: When you can't move, you can look around by dragging the mouse!!
